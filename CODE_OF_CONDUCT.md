# Community code of conduct

Flekks Medical Workspace is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and clinically responsible community.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and kind.
- Respect clinical, technical, accessibility, and privacy expertise.
- Separate critique of an idea or patch from criticism of a person.
- Protect patient privacy and never request or post real patient data.
- Disclose conflicts of interest and uncertainty when discussing clinical or security claims.
- Help newcomers find bounded, testable contribution paths.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and deliberate privacy violations are not tolerated.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Security or privacy vulnerabilities should use the private process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors when needed to protect the community.

This policy is informed by the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
